## Why This Tool?

Moving into a Japanese apartment and don't know how to turn on the utilities? This guide walks you through each service in plain English with the Japanese phrases you'll need.

## Features

- **5 utility types** — Electricity, gas, water, internet, and mobile phone
- **Step-by-step instructions** — Follow along from start to finish
- **Japanese phrases included** — Know exactly what to say when calling providers
- **Estimated costs** — Budget for your monthly utility bills
- **Provider information** — Links and contact details for major providers

## How It Works

1. Pick a utility (Electricity, Gas, Water, Internet, or Mobile)
2. Follow the step-by-step setup guide
3. Use the included Japanese phrases when contacting providers
